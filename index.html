My Biography

Hello, my name is Touhar Fang. As the fourth child of six children and the oldest son, I am one of two who have chosen the academic pathway. I truly hope that after completing this journey by obtaining a Bachelor of Science in Business Administration with an emphasis in Computer Information System, I will be a role model for my children as well as my other siblings. As a lost and confused first generation child, it has taken me longer to achieve my objective, because the first half of my college experience was full of uncertainty and lack of direction. However, I am happy to say that this will be my last year here at Fresno State, and I will finish my senior year strong.
